About

I am a student at Michigan State University pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Media Information and Technology. I'm interested in basically any and all areas of creative projects.
I have experience with the following programs: Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Lightroom, Dreamweaver (HTML, CSS, JS), Flash, Maya 3D, and Avid Media Composer 6.5
To me, creation is such a pleasing activity. There is a sense of progress and achievement I'm filled with every time I see a project start to come together.
